Old Fashioned

LEMON BARS

45MIN

PREPARATION
  1. Preheat 350 degrees F
  2. Combined flour, baking soda, spices and salt and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together shortening and lemon juice and zest.
  4. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients. Stir in raisins.
  5. Evenly spread batter in a lightly grease 9 inch baking pan.
  6. Bake 25-30 min. until top is lightly colored. Cool in pan on rack. Cut into bars
INGREDIENTS
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/5 tsp. salt
  • 1/5 tsp. shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp. fresh lemon zest
  • 1 cup raisins
